Journal

Hear from students and travelers about the educational impact of our tour.

"The University of the Philippines (UP) has several Technology Business Incubators (TBIs) that support IT‑focused and technology startups by providing practical, real‑world business development resources and mentorship. These incubators are part of UP’s broader mission to promote innovation, entrepreneurship, and technology commercialization across the Philippines."

"Visiting Rivan IT (Rivan School of Technology) opened my eyes to what professional IT training looks like. As a student used to classroom learning, seeing their training labs filled with real Cisco routers, switches, and enterprise equipment was impressive - this is what the real world uses! The instructors shared their experiences working abroad and how proper training and certifications opened doors for them globally. They emphasized that Filipino IT professionals are respected worldwide, but we need the right skills and credentials to compete."

"Experiencing MATA Technologies’ VR and 360° tours gave me a glimpse into the future of technology. As a student focused on coding, seeing VR’s applications in real estate and tourism was eye-opening. Their virtual tour of Lubang Island made me feel present on-site, while showcasing how technology can preserve Philippine culture and promote local tourism. The team’s passion for using innovation to support Filipino businesses was truly inspiring.

"Today, I visited the Telephone and Radio System Integrated Emergency Response (TaRSIER 117) in Bohol. As an IT student, witnessing this critical infrastructure in action was enlightening, showing how technology directly saves lives. I was impressed by the seamless integration of landline, mobile, radio, and VoIP communications, and how operators efficiently coordinate emergency responses across the province for medical and disaster situations. "
